dryrbis a collection of micro-libraries, each intended to encapsulate a common task in Ruby.

It was born out of frustration at the state of third-party libraries available in the Ruby ecosystem, namely their tendency to be written in the typical “idiomatic” Ruby way.

Libraries belonging to the dryrb organisation try to follow a few basic principles:
